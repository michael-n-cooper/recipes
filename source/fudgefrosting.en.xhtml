Chocolate Fudge Frosting Frostings 8 oz dark chocolate chopped 1 cup heavy cream Scald cream. Pour cream over chocolate, and mix until the chocolate melts and the mixture is smooth. Let cool to room temperature. Beat mixture briefly for a dense frosting, until it lightens slightly for a smooth frosting, or until quite light and mounted for a fluffy frosting. Use immediatly to ice a cake. Store in the refrigerator. Return to room temperature and rebeat before using.
